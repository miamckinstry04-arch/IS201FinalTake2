About Me

This is what I do.

Hi, I'm Mia McKinstry — a Marketing student at Brigham Young University's Marriott School of Business with a passion for storytelling, content creation, and human connection. I believe that great marketing isn't just about selling a product; it's about building genuine relationships with people.

My background spans content creation, photography, language tutoring, and customer service — experiences that have shaped me into a well-rounded communicator who understands how to connect with audiences across different contexts and platforms. Whether I'm crafting a short-form video ad or coaching a new team member at work, I bring creativity, dedication, and a genuine care for people to everything I do.

Outside of school and work, I love spending time with my family, exploring new places, and finding beauty in everyday moments. I'm driven by a desire to make a positive impact — on the brands I work with, the people I serve, and the world around me. I'm always looking for opportunities to learn, grow, and contribute something meaningful.
